# Epic: Contact and Terms

## US_31 — Contact and Terms Links

- **Story:** As a user, I want access to contact details and terms of service so I can report issues or review service conditions.
- **Acceptance Criteria (AC):**
  - [ ] App footer on every page includes “Contact” link (mailto: configured email address).
  - [ ] Footer includes “Terms” link leading to terms of service page.
  - [ ] Footer links are visible for both authenticated and unauthenticated users.
- **Priority:** Could
